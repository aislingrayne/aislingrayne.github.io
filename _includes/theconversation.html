When rehoming wildlife, Indigenous leadership delivers the best results

Moving plants and animals to establish new populations or strengthen existing ones can enhance species recovery and ecosystem resilience. But few projects are led or co-led by Indigenous Peoples, or centre Indigenous ways of knowing. In this article for The Conversation, we explain why we need transformative change that brings together diverse knowledge and worldviews to restore ecosystems as well as cultural practices and language.
